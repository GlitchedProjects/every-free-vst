# What is Every Free VST?
**Every Free VST** is a website where you can find, well, every free VST!
Including VST-I, VST-FX, VST2, VST3, Clap, and pretty much any virtual instrument or effect I can find.

My goal is to make finding VSTs easier for everyone by having solid search features, with things like categories, tags, and a search bar so you can pinpoint exactly what you need or want.
You can also save your hard earned money by finding **free** alternatives to paid plugins through an easy on the eyes UI and solid search features instead of a blinding white boring ol' spreadsheet.

# Why
My brother has always made music as a hobby, and he introduced me to the world of virtual synthesis and instrument simulation with VSTs. They had always fascinated me, but I thought they were like real instruments in which you had to pay for every single one, like we did with our first 3 VSTs.
It wasn't until a couple years later, I start messing around in various DAWs trying to make music for my own projects, and the switch from FL to Ableton Live Starter was nice, but there were WAY less synths and effects.
So one night while I'm learning Ableton I come across a video titled: "9 Free Plugins (That Shouldn't Be Free)" by Venus Theory, and I was hooked, It honestly felt like getting whole new instruments and a brand new world of sound for **FREE**.
Since then I've built a catalogue of my favorite free Synths and Effects, as well as sources I go to if I want a new free VSTs.
